ShellExecuteEx function.

Fill the SHELLEXECUTEINFO fields you need, and leave the others as default. The needed mask flags will be automatically set.

§Examples

use winsafe::{self as w, prelude::*, co};

w::ShellExecuteEx(&w::SHELLEXECUTEINFO {
    file: "C:\\Temp\\foo.exe",
    show: co::SW::SHOW,
    ..Default::default()
})?;
